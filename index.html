How SkillConnect Works

1

Create & Share Your Profile

Sign up securely with Supabase auth and publish what skills you can teach or want to learn.

2

Propose Skill Exchange

Browse local mentors on the map or feed and send a direct skill exchange request.

3

Connect, Learn & Review

Collaborate via real-time DM chat, meet for sessions, and leave star reviews for each other.
