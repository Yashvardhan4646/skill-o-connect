Empowering Communities Through Skills

SkillConnect bridges the gap between learners and mentors in your neighborhood — discover people who share, teach, and grow together.

About SkillConnect

SkillConnect is a community platform designed to foster collaboration and skill exchange. Whether you want to learn or share your expertise, we bring people together for real-world skill growth.

Discover Local Talent

Find nearby users sharing their skills. Whether it's a coding mentor, fitness trainer, or art teacher — SkillConnect helps you connect with the right people locally.

Chat & Collaborate

Connect directly through messages, ask questions, share progress, or plan meetups. Collaboration starts with conversation — SkillConnect makes that effortless.

Share What You Know

Post your own skill — describe what you teach or create. Inspire others to learn and grow. Be a part of your community’s collective growth journey.

Interactive Map

See everyone around you on a live map — find mentors and learners in your area. The map uses live location and real-time updates for a smooth experience.

Built for Viksit Bharat Buildathon

This project was proudly developed as part of the Viksit Bharat Buildathon — an initiative encouraging innovation, collaboration, and digital empowerment across India. SkillConnect embodies the spirit of a self-reliant India by empowering local communities to learn and share skills through technology.
